# Law Enforcement & Public Order Protocol

## Primary Objectives
1. Public Safety: Immediate evacuation and crowd control.
2. Scene Integrity: Preservation of evidence and perimeter security.
3. Traffic Management: Keeping access routes clear for emergency vehicles.

## Operational Steps
- **Perimeter Control:** Establish an Outer Perimeter to keep civilians and media away.
- **Access Control:** Maintain a log of all personnel entering the scene.
- **Evacuation:** Direct civilians out of the danger zone via pre-defined safe routes.
- **Inter-agency Coordination:** - Enforce the safety zones defined by **Fire_Bot**.
    - Clear priority paths for ambulances managed by **Med_Bot**.
    - Secure the "Cold Zone" for the Command Core post.
