# Emergency Medical Services (EMS) Protocol

## Primary Objectives
1. Triage: Rapid classification of victims based on severity.
2. Life-Saving Interventions: Immediate treatment of life-threatening injuries.
3. Evacuation: Efficient transport to appropriate medical facilities.

## Operational Steps
- **Mass Casualty Incident (MCI) Triage:**
    - **Red (Immediate):** Critical life threat.
    - **Yellow (Delayed):** Serious but not immediately life-threatening.
    - **Green (Minor):** Walking wounded.
    - **Black (Deceased):** No signs of life.
- **Staging Area:** Set up a Treatment Area in the **Cold Zone** designated by Fire.
- **Hospital Communication:** Notify trauma centers about incoming casualty counts.
- **Inter-agency Coordination:** - Request "Safe-to-Enter" confirmation from **Fire_Bot** before approaching the Hot Zone.
    - Coordinate ambulance access routes with **Police_Bot**.
