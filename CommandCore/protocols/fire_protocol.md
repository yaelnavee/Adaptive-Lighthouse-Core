# Fire Department & Rescue Operational Protocol

## Primary Objectives
1. Life Safety: Immediate rescue of trapped individuals.
2. Incident Stabilization: Containment of fire, gas leaks, or hazardous materials.
3. Property & Environment: Minimizing damage once lives are secured.

## Operational Steps
- **Scene Assessment:** Identify fuel sources (chemical, electrical, gas).
- **Zoning:** - Establish a **Hot Zone** (immediate danger, specialized gear only).
    - Establish a **Warm Zone** (decontamination and support).
    - Establish a **Cold Zone** (command post and medical staging).
- **Resource Management:** Deploy water curtains or foam based on fire type.
- **Inter-agency Coordination:** - Provide the safe perimeter distance to **Police_Bot**.
    - Inform **Med_Bot** of the number and status of rescued victims.
